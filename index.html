About AJ Sheehan

Thanks for visiting my page today!

Since I graduated Indiana University in 2010, I have been a sales and management professional working at companies such as Salesforce and Enterprise Rent A Car. I have and always will enjoy the art of management, most noteably seeing others succeed. In the sales world, however, I always felt there was something missing. It took me a long time, but I finally figured out it was my love for solving problems and building projects.

At the beginning of 2020, with the birth of my son, I began to look internally to determine what it is that I would love to do, and this internal search led me to the Eleven Fifty Academy.
